🎨 Graduation Thesis & Design

Lost Ruins - Akrosa: 3D Game Environment Design

(《失落遗迹-阿克洛萨》三维游戏场景设计)

Awarded the Outstanding Graduation Thesis/Design at Shanghai Jian Qiao University. This project focuses on the comprehensive creation of a high-quality 3D game environment using Unreal Engine 5. It encompasses the full PBR pipeline from concept art and 3D modeling to texturing, material creation, and advanced scene lighting optimization.

Research on the Application of Maqiao Culture in Adventure Puzzle Games

(《马桥文化在冒险解谜游戏中的应用研究》)

Master's Graduation Thesis & Project: This research explores the digital revitalization of the Maqiao cultural heritage. By integrating traditional archaeological elements into an interactive 3D adventure puzzle game format, the project aims to enhance player engagement and promote the preservation of "cold heritage" through modern game design methodologies and narrative integration.
